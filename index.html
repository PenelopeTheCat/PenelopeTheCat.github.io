Deena Burnette

Deena Burnette is a native of Middletown where she started dancing at the age of 3. Since childhood she has performed and competed throughout the tri-state area and Pennsylvania. Miss Deena has been sharing her passion for dance and movement with students at the Middletown Arts Center for the past 8 years. She loves inspiring children to move in the studio and learn in the classroom. She has choreographed, directed, and produced shows for Sesame Place, Hershey Park, The Hershey Bears, The Lakewood Blue Claws, The Somerset Patriots, The Middletown Talent Show, and numerous productions for local nursing homes and assisted living facilities.
